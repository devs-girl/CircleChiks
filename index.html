About

Clara Melo, was born in Angola and spent his childhood in Lousã, a small village in the sunny Portugal. She did is Bachelor of Design at the Creative University in Lisbon, IADE. For a few years worked as an interior designer. As a creative, she wanted more and decide to start his own Fashion Brand, a Portuguese Fashion brand that puts if focus on African fabrics. Whith two successful Fashion stores, one in Luanda and another in Lisbon, participations on fashion week events, and styling a few TV presenters and musicians.
